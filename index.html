Introduction

I'm Daniel Dafoe. I'm honest, enthusiastic, optimistic, hard-working, and quick-witted. Adjectives aside, what you'll notice first about me is that my ears perk up when I discuss web development, usability, and the English language—and even higher if there's pizza involved.

Fun facts: I write stories, contribute to open source, write a cool blog, and make some money selling stickers and clothing I design.

Doesn't Run on Batteries

When it comes to getting work done, Daniel doesn't run on AA batteries. Instead, he runs on a lithium ion rechargeable battery. This kind of upgrade means that he can work harder and longer than alkaline-based developers, and, at times, only requires a quick recharge (🍕) before finishing an arduous task.
