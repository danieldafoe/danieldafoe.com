Web & Mobile Developer

I'm Daniel Dafoe. I'm honest, determined, enthusiastic, optimistic, hard-working and quick-witted. Adjectives aside, I am someone who gets excited talking about web development and design, and is fascinated by the technology surrounding mobile application development.

Fun facts: I write articles and stories on Medium, I contributed an open source CSS library; designed, developed and began writing a blog; and even made some money selling an SQL inspired sticker I designed.

From Canada's largest bank to small marketing companies, I've been a part of a radically diverse set of teams which have given me a well-rounded perspective of designing and developing web experiences that meet business goals and objectives.

Furthermore, with education in Advertising and Systems Analysis, I've been exposed to a fantastic array of disciplines such as creative copywriting, software development, business analysis, and interactive user interface design.

My passion is building aesthetically pleasing and intuitive experiences for users of the world wide web. I'm constantly absorbed by the possibilities of the web, and I love trying out what's trendy in the realm of front end web development.

When I'm not reading about web design or mobile trends, you can find me reading literature pre 1900s, writing short stories, watching a play, or working on my personal fitness.
